# Core Principles / 核心原则

## English

- Start from user task and business value.
- Do not assume AI is the right answer.
- Separate facts, assumptions, open questions, and next actions.
- Tie every function to a pain point and measurable value.
- Tie every AI capability to input, processing, output, limits, and fallback.
- Tie every Agent action to permissions, approval points, state, recovery, and auditability.
- Tie every data item to analysis, safety, evaluation, or operations value.

## 中文

- 从用户任务和业务价值出发，不从模型能力倒推。
- 不要默认 AI 一定适合这个问题。
- 明确区分事实、假设、开放问题和下一步动作。
- 每个功能都要回到具体痛点和可衡量价值。
- 每个 AI 能力都要写清输入、处理、输出、边界和兜底。
- 每个 Agent 动作都要写清权限、确认点、状态、恢复和审计。
- 每个数据项都要对应分析、安全、评估或运营价值。
